About Me

Born and raised in London.

Since 2016, I've lived in Tokyo, Seoul and Fukuoka while working as an English teacher. While looking for a career change/ I do courses online and so...?, I stumbled upon CS50 on edX and from there discovered the world of coding. I began teaching myself HTML, CSS and Javascript using other free online resources such as Codecademy, FreeCodeCamp, Flat Iron School's prep course and The Odin Project.

I enjoy being creative and love the euphoric feeling you get when, after you've been trying to get your code to work for days, it finally works! Since getting into coding a few months ago, it has become my new hobby? Thing I like.

I love travelling, exploring new places, reading and doing other things that will make me interesting.
